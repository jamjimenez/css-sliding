Revealing CSS3 Menu

The page navigation is revealed when you move your mouse or touch the left-hand edge of the web page. It could be ideal for mobile or tablet devices where space is at a premium.

The page uses HTML5 and CSS3 transitions. No JavaScript is required.

This demonstration works in most browsers. Older applications will not show all effects but remain operational.
